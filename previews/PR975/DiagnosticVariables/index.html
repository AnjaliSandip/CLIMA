CliMA Diagnostic Variable List

This document contains the diagnostic variables in CliMA. Format: short name (code name) = long name.

LES Diagnostics

default

rho(ρ) = density
u(u) = x_velocity
v(v) = y_velocity
w(w) = z_velocity
qt(q_tot) = total specific humidity
et(e_tot) = total specific energy
qv(q_vap) = water vapor specific humidity
ql(q_liq) = liquid water specific humidity
ei(e_int) = specific internal energy
thd(θ_dry) = potential temperature
thl(θ_liq_ice) = liquid-ice potential temperature
thv(θ_vir) = virtual potential temperature
hm(h_moi) = specific enthalpy
ht(h_tot) = total specific enthalpy

var_u(u′u′) = variance of x-velocity
var_v(v′v′) = variance of y-velocity
var_w(w'w') = variance of z-velocity
TKE(TKE) = turbulence kinetic energy
var_qt(q_tot'q_tot') = variance of total specific humidity
var_ei(e_int'e_int') = variance of specific internal energy
var_thl(θ_liq_ice'θ_liq_ice') = variance of liquid-ice potential temperature
w3(w′w′w′) = the third moment of z-velocity

cov_qt_thl(q_tot'θ_liq_ice') = covariance of total specific humidity and liquid-ice potential temperature
cov_qt_ei(q_tot'e_int') = covariance of total specific humidity and specific internal energy
cov_w_rho(w′ρ′) = vertical eddy flux of mass
cov_w_u(w′u′) = vertical eddy flux of x-velocity
cov_w_v(w′v′) = vertical eddy flux of y-velocity
w_qt(wq_tot) = vertical flux of total specific humidity
cov_w_qt(w'q_tot') = vertical eddy flux of total specific humidity
cov_w_qv(w′q_vap′) = vertical eddy flux of water vapor specific humidity
cov_w_ql(w′q_liq′) = vertical eddy flux of liuqid water specific humidity
cov_w_thd(w′θ_dry′) = vertical eddy flux of potential temperature
cov_w_thv(w′θ_vir′) = vertical eddy flux of virtual temperature
cov_w_thl(w′θ_liq_ice′) = vertical eddy flux of liquid-ice potential temperature
cov_w_ei(w′e_int′) = vertical eddy flux of specific internal energy
w_qt_sgs(d_q_tot) = vertical sgs flux of total specific humidity
w_ht_sgs(d_h_tot) = vertical sgs flux of total specific enthalpy

cld_frac(cld_frac) = cloud fraction
cld_cover(cld_cover) = cloud cover
cld_top(cld_top) = cloud cover
cld_base(cld_base) = cloud base
lwp(lwp) = liquid water path

core

rho_core(ρ_core) = cloud core density
u_core(u_core) = cloud core x_velocity
v_core(v_core) = cloud core y_velocity
w_core(w_core) = cloud core z_velocity
qt_core(q_tot_core) = cloud core total specific humidity
ei_core(e_int_core) = cloud core specific internal energy
thl_core(θ_liq_ice) = cloud core liquid-ice potential temperature

var_u_core(u′u′_core) = cloud core variance of x-velocity
var_v_core(v′v′_core) = cloud core variance of y-velocity
var_w_core(w'w'_core) = cloud core variance of z-velocity
var_qt_core(q_tot'q_tot'_core) = cloud core variance of total specific humidity
var_ei_core(e_int'e_int'_core) = cloud core variance of specific internal energy
var_thl_core(θ_liq_ice'θ_liq_ice'_core) = cloud core variance of liquid-ice potential temperature

cov_qt_thl_core(q_tot'θ_liq_ice'_core) = cloud core covariance of total specific humidity and liquid-ice potential temperature
cov_qt_ei_core(q_tot'e_int'_core) = cloud core covariance of total specific humidity and specific internal energy
cov_w_rho_core(w′ρ′_core) = cloud core vertical eddy flux of mass
cov_w_qt_core(w'q_tot'_core) = cloud core vertical eddy flux of total specific humidity
cov_w_thl_core(w′θ_liq_ice′_core) = cloud core vertical eddy flux of liquid-ice potential temperature
cov_w_ei_core(w′e_int′_core) = cloud core vertical eddy flux of specific internal energy

core_frac(core_frac) = cloud core (q_liq > 0 and w > 0) fraction
